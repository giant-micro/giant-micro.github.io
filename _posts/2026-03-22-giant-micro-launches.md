Today we launched Giant Micro — an AI integration company based in Blue Earth, Minnesota.

We believe every small business deserves the tools that used to be reserved for enterprises. The technology is here. The cost is right. The only thing missing is someone to put it all together.

That's what we do. We integrate AI into your business operations — email, scheduling, data management, customer communication — so you can focus on what matters.

Our first deployment: Blue Earth Cooperative Bakery, a local cottage bakery that now has its own AI agent managing Google Workspace. Deployed in under a week on open-source infrastructure.

We're just getting started. If you're a small business looking to compete like a giant, we'd love to talk.
