What Is MicroClaw?

Every AI agent starts each conversation from scratch. It doesn't remember last week's email. It doesn't know your busiest day. Every interaction is a first interaction.

Our agents are built on OpenClaw, the fastest-growing open-source AI agent platform in the world. OpenClaw gives our agents their tools, their connections to your systems, and their ability to act. But out of the box, every agent wakes up with amnesia.

MicroClaw is our proprietary memory architecture built on top of OpenClaw. It's the reason our agents feel like team members instead of chatbots. They remember. Not just facts, but context, priorities, and where things were headed.

The Problem

Most AI systems treat every conversation as isolated. They can answer questions and complete tasks, but they can't build on yesterday's work. They can't notice patterns over weeks. They can't remember that your vendor changed their delivery schedule last month.

For a business, that's the difference between a tool and a team member. A tool does what you tell it, every time, from zero. A team member knows the business, anticipates needs, and gets better over time.

Without memory, AI is a tool. With MicroClaw, it's a team member.

How MicroClaw Works

1

Identity

Your agent knows who it is, who you are, and how your business works. This foundation persists across every interaction. It never has to be re-taught.

2

Context

Relationships, projects, and business state are tracked in structured layers. Your agent wakes up knowing where things stand, not starting from a blank page.

3

Direction

Your agent remembers not just what happened, but where things were headed. Open tasks, priorities, and next steps carry forward automatically.

What This Means for Your Business

Your agent remembers that Tuesdays are your busiest day. It knows your top vendor's contact info changed last week. It remembers the marketing idea you mentioned in passing three weeks ago and follows up when the timing is right.

It's not magic. It's architecture. And it gets better with every version.
