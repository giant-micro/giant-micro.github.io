Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Who We Are

Giant Micro ("we," "us," "our") provides managed AI agent services for small businesses. Our website is giant-micro.com. You can reach us at hello@giant-micro.com.

What We Collect

Website visitors: We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts on this website. We do not collect personal information from visitors unless you voluntarily submit it through our contact form.

Contact form submissions: If you use our contact form, we receive your name, email address, and message. This information is processed by Formspree and delivered to us via email. We use it only to respond to your inquiry.

Clients: If you become a client, we process business information necessary to operate your AI agent, including email content, calendar data, and business documents you authorize us to access. This data is stored on a dedicated server provisioned exclusively for your business. We do not share, sell, or use client data for any purpose other than providing the service you purchased.

Third-Party Services

Our services may interact with third-party platforms on your behalf, including:

  • Google Workspace (email, calendar, documents)
  • Slack (messaging)
  • Facebook and Instagram (social media management)
  • GitHub (website hosting)

We access these platforms only with your explicit authorization and only to perform the services you requested. Each platform's own privacy policy governs their handling of your data.

How We Protect Your Data

  • Each client gets a dedicated, isolated server. Your data is never mixed with another client's data.
  • Access to client systems is restricted to authorized Giant Micro personnel.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal or business data with third parties.
  • We do not use client data to train AI models.

Data Retention

We retain client data for the duration of the service agreement. If you cancel your service, we will delete your dedicated server and all associated data within 30 days of cancellation, unless you request an export first.

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Request an export of your data before cancellation

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@giant-micro.com.

Children

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We will not materially reduce your privacy protections without notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@giant-micro.com.
