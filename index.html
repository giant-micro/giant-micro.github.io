What we stand for

A handful of values run everything we do. They are not slogans on a wall, they are how we decide.

Honesty over hype

We would rather lose a deal than oversell what AI can do. If a tool is not worth it, you will hear that from us first.

Always getting better

We are always sharpening our own tools and methods. When you work with us, you come along on that journey, and your business keeps improving as we do.

You own what we build

Everything we hand over is plain, organized, and human-readable. No black boxes. You can open it, understand it, and change it.

Your data stays yours

We work inside your own accounts. We advise and build; we never hold or sell your data.

We are customer zero

We run Giant Micro on the very tools and methods we sell you. If we would not use it ourselves, we will not sell it to you.

Lean by design

We build only what earns its keep, sized to what your team can actually run after we leave. No bloat, no busywork.
