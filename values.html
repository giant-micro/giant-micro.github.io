Core Values

These aren't aspirations. They're how we operate. Every decision we make runs through these principles.

🎯

Honesty Over Hype

The AI industry is full of promises that don't hold up. We only claim what's running. Every capability on our site is deployed and working today. If it's not ready, it's on our roadmap, not our sales pitch.

🤝

Partnership, Not Projects

We don't build something and leave. Your agent grows with your business, and we grow with you. This is a relationship, not a transaction.

🛠️

Competence Over Credentials

20 years in IT. Not in a lab. In the gap between humans and technology, making things work for real people. We earn trust by doing the work, not by selling the slide deck.

📈

Continuous Improvement

Your agent gets better every week. So do we. When new capabilities emerge, your business gets them. Stagnation is not in our vocabulary.

🍽️

Customer Zero

Giant Micro runs on the same tools we deploy for you. Our website, our email, our operations are all managed by our own AI agents. Every problem you might hit, we've already found and fixed.

🔒

Classified
